Why Bother Learning French?

French is a hard language to learn for english speaking people, and it has complicated rules so why learning it?

  • French is a beautiful, rich, and melodious language; it is even called the language of love.
  • It is a language taught in the education system of most countries around the world.
  • French is a good base for learning other Romance languages.
  • It is one of the very few languages spoken all over the world.
  • Knowing French offers access to great works of literature, as well as French movies and songs.
  • The French language is useful when traveling to places such as Africa, Switzerland, Monaco, the Seychelles, etc.
  • The ability of speaking French opens up advantages in the international job market and study opportunities in French universities and business schools.
And if this isn't enough:
  • France is the world's number-one tourist destination.
  • French is the international language of cooking, fashion, theatre, the visual arts, dance and architecture.
